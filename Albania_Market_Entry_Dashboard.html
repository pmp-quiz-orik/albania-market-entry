Executive Summary

Key findings for NLB Group Board
  • GDP growing at 4.0% (2024), Lek appreciating to 97.9/EUR (strongest in 25 years) signals economic confidence
  • Banking sector EUR 19.2B assets with only 53% loan-to-deposit ratio — massive untapped lending capacity
  • Tourism tripled from 1.2M to 4.2M accommodation nights (2018-2025); border arrivals hit 11.7M in 2024
  • Mortgage penetration ~7-8% of GDP vs EU average 30-40% — largest single product opportunity
  • Construction boomed to 1,989k m² in 2024 (Tirana 89% share), though 2025 shows cooling (-57% YoY)
Albania offers a compelling entry opportunity for NLB Group. A mid-tier bank acquisition ($150-250M) targeting mortgage, SME, and diaspora segments could achieve 12-15% ROE by Year 5 with a top-5 market position.

Urbanization Trends

  • Tirana metro accounts for ~35% of population and 89% of construction permits (2024)
  • Durres-Tirana corridor emerging as continuous urban zone with combined ~45% of population
  • Coastal cities (Vlore, Saranda) growing via tourism-driven development
  • Rural depopulation continues — 3-4% annual decline in northern prefectures

Labor Market

  • Unemployment trending down from ~14% (2020) to ~11% (2024)
  • Youth unemployment remains elevated at ~22%
  • Emigration drains skilled labor — estimated 30,000-40,000 leave annually
  • Minimum wage increased to ALL 40,000/month (~EUR 410) in 2024
Source: INSTAT Labor Force Survey, World Bank Development Indicators

Growth Drivers

  • Rising property prices — Tirana +72%, coast +100% in 5 years = larger mortgages
  • Community residence boom — INSTAT shows 16x increase in residential permits (2020-2024)
  • Diaspora demand — 1.6M Albanians abroad invest in homeland properties; need EUR mortgages
  • Rate environment — Housing at 5.5%, declining as BoA eases; affordable for growing middle class
  • Low penetration — 4x room for growth vs EU average mortgage-to-GDP ratio

Mortgage Sizing

  • Current market: EUR 2.1B outstanding
  • Potential at EU average: EUR 7.5-10.0B (3.5-5x current)
  • NLB target (10% share by Yr 5): EUR 750M-1.0B mortgage book

NLB Strategic Opportunity

Why NLB can win in Albania

1. Mortgage & Housing Finance

  • EUR-denominated mortgages for diaspora buyers
  • 4x market growth potential (7%→30% of GDP)
  • Rising prices = larger loan sizes = higher margins

2. SME & Tourism Lending

  • Tourism SMEs under-served; hotel construction booming
  • NLB's SME platform from SI/MK directly transferable
  • Working capital + investment loans for hospitality sector

3. Digital-First Banking

  • NLB Klik = best-in-class digital platform in SEE
  • Albania at 55% internet banking vs 85% EU = 30pp gap
  • Young population (median 38.6) is digital-ready

4. Diaspora Corridors & Deposits

  • $2.3B remittances (8.4% GDP) — NLB operates in key source markets
  • Cross-border transfer products + FX services
  • Low-cost deposit gathering from diaspora accounts
NLB's regional SEE footprint (SI, MK, KS, RS, ME), digital platform, and EUR operations make Albania a natural next market. Acquisition of a 5-8% player creates an immediate beachhead with synergies from Day 1.
